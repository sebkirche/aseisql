Returns a char where a certain part of a string (char_expr1) is replaced by another string (char_expr2).

stuff(char_expr1, start, length, char_expr2)

Parameter information :
char_expr1 : A variable, constant or column of datatype char or varchar (null or not null) or of unichar or univarchar.
start : The starting postion within char_expr1 of the characters that should be deleted.
length : To length of the part to delete from char_expr1.
char_expr2 : A variable, constant or column of datatype char or varchar (null or not null) or of unichar or univarchar.
