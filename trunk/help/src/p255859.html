Add or change a password for a login.

sp_password caller_password, new_password [, loginame ,immediate]

Parameter information :
caller_password : Your current password.
new_password : The new password.
loginame : Optionally the name of the login for which the password must change.
immediate : specifies whether a password immediately takes effect on users who are logged in Possible values: 0 : users who are logged in keep their old passwords until they reconnect, 1 : the password changes immediately in the syslogins table, and users who are logged in get their passwords updated while they are still logged in.
