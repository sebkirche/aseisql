Compare two string with alternate collating rules.

result = compare (char_expression1, char_expression2 [, {collation_name | collation_ID}])

Parameter information :
result : The returned value. Possible values: -1 : char_expression1 < char_expression2, 0 : char_expression1 = char_expression2, 1 : char_expression1 > char_expression2
char_expression1 : A variable, constant or column of datatype char or varchar (null or not null) or of unichar or univarchar.
char_expression2 : A variable, constant or column of datatype char or varchar (null or not null) or of unichar or univarchar.
collation_name : The name of a collation sequence.
collation_ID : The id of a collation_sequence.
